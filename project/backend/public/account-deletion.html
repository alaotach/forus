Delete Your Account

Complete both verification checks to permanently delete your account and associated data. This action cannot be undone.

What this flow checks

1) Your account email + password

2) A one-time code sent to your email

If you cannot access your email, contact support@forus.eryzalabs.com.

1

Verify identity with email and password

2
