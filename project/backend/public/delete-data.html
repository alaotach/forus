For Us - Data Deletion Request

Developer: Alaotach / For Us

This page explains how users can request deletion of account data for the For Us app.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Email support@forus.eryzalabs.com from your registered account email.
  2. Use subject: Delete my data.
  3. Include your account email and (optional) couple code for faster processing.

Data Deleted

  • Account profile information
  • Chat messages and related metadata
  • Daily writing entries, diary entries, and shared notes
  • Uploaded media and related metadata
  • Push notification tokens

Data That May Be Retained Temporarily

  • Security and abuse-prevention logs (up to 30 days)
  • Data required by law or compliance obligations
  • Encrypted backups (removed within 30 days)

Deletion Timeline

  • Request acknowledgment: usually within 3 business days
  • Primary deletion from active systems: within 7 days after confirmation
  • Backup lifecycle deletion: within 30 days
